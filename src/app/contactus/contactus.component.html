We would love for you to Exhibit or have a Table with us here at SpiritCon!

Vendors will be in a room separate from Readers and Healers to give better atmosphere to both.

  • Vendor booths are 10'x10' and $120.
  • They have the option to do split booths that are 5'x10' for only $60.
  • Reader booths are 6'x6' for $60 and Healer booths are 10'x10' for $80.
  • All booths come with tables, tablecloths, and chairs.
  • We also have info booths that are just a 4' table with tablecloth. These are free to any groups wishing to have one.
