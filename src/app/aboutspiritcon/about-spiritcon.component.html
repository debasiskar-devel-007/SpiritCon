March 20th and 21st 2021 join us for 2 incredible days of Pagan spirit, magic, healing and community!

SpiritCon was born from the need to have a local place for a witchy, Pagan, metaphysical,
and healing oriented individual to participate in community and support structures.

There is very little awareness to how large and diverse this of the individual, groups and community
at large. We aim to "open dialogue" and build trust and connections to better lead, guide and
enhance the spiritual lives of those identifying under the "Pagan" banner

SpiritCon is a safe place for Pagans, spiritual seekers and healers to gather in communion for learning, sharing and celebrating together.

Information is something that makes a very large impact in the lives of Pagans and spiritual seekers alike. Coming together to share in education, skill development and share in each other’s unique gifts and ideas can be a very empowering experience.

We want to explore new and ancient horizons together and inspire each other to live in the Pagan and spiritual lifestyles together. Most of all we are very supportive of developing families, communities, organizations and we are all coming together to have fun and explore!

We would love for you to Exhibit or have a Table with us here at SpiritCon!

Vendors will be in a room separate from Readers and Healers to give better atmosphere to both.

  • Vendor booths are 10'x10' and $120.
  • They have the option to do split booths that are 5'x10' for only $60.
  • Reader booths are 6'x6' for $60 and Healer booths are 10'x10' for $80.
  • All booths come with tables, tablecloths, and chairs.
  • We also have info booths that are just a 4' table with tablecloth. These are free to any groups wishing to have one.
