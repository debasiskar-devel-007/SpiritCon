CLASSES THAT WILL BE OFFERED AT OUR TWO-DAY EVENT

  • Craft classes include Everyday Crafting, Flat Magic (Paper Crafts), Altars, and Crystal Grids.
  • Energy Classes include Empathic Defense, Chi Gung, Chakras, and Dance Chant Magic.
  • Nature classes include Animals (Totems and More), Plants, Rocks/Crystals, and Mythical Creatures.
  • Cycles classes include Wheel of the Year, Lunar, Mysteries, and Places of Power.
  • Traditions classes include Greek/Roman, Celtic, Egyptian, Norse, Root Magic, Intro to Qabala, and Beginning Devotional Practices.
  • Divination classes include Tarot reading, Tea Leaf reading, Casting bones, Astrology, Runes, Pendulum, and Sigils.

WE WILL HAVE A VENDOR ROOM FULL OF CRYSTALS, WOOD BURNED ITEMS, HAND TOOLED LEATHER WORKS, JEWELRY, HANDMADE CRAFTS, SOAPS, CANDLES, AND MORE TO AID IN YOUR SPIRITUAL JOURNEY.

There will also be a reader and healer room for Tarot, Medium, and Psychic readings, as well as Reiki.
